Preparing Throwing Returning Practice

1. Preparing Your Yo-Yo

  • Ensure the string is the correct length (from your hand to your waist).
  • Tie a loop at the end of the string for your finger.
  • Wrap the string neatly around the yo-yo.

2. Learning the Basic Throw

  • Slip the loop over your middle finger, halfway down your finger.
  • Hold the yo-yo in your palm with your palm facing up.
  • Throw the yo-yo downward smoothly, letting the string unwind completely.

3. Bringing the Yo-Yo Back

  • As the yo-yo reaches the end of the string, give a gentle tug.
  • Control the timing and strength of your tug to bring it back smoothly.

4. Practice Makes Perfect

Start with simple tricks like The Sleeper or Walk the Dog. As you improve, explore advanced tricks to impress your friends!

Remember: patience and practice are key to mastering the yo-yo. Enjoy the journey!
